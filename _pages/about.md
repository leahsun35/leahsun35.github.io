I am currently a Master of Science in Economics student at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE). My primary academic focus lies at the intersection of Labor Economics, Family Economics, and Macroeconomics. I also have a strong background in Applied Econometrics, frequently utilizing Difference-in-Differences (DiD) and synthetic control methods for policy evaluation, alongside exploring New Keynesian models.

## Education
* **M.S. in Economics**, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (Sep 2024 - Present)
* **B.S. in Economics**, Anhui Normal University (Sep 2018 - Jun 2022) 

## Research Interests

* **Secondary Themes:** Applied Econometrics, Demographic Economics, International Macro-Spillovers.

## Research Experience
